Privacy Policy


Last updated: 26/07/2023


Thank you for participating in our social sports league. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your data when you register for the league through our online form. By providing your information, you consent to the practices outlined in this policy.


1. Information We Collect


We collect the following personal information through our online form:


  • Name

  • Mobile Number

  • Email

  • Age

  • Gender

  • Name of Friend


2. Use of Information


We will use the collected information for the following purposes:


  • Facilitating team formation and matching players based on their preferences, ages, and genders.

  • Creating team WhatsApp groups to enable effective communication among teammates.

  • Sending emails about team fixtures, league standings, and other relevant league updates.


3. Data Sharing


We do not share your personal information with anyone, including third parties. Your data is kept confidential and will only be accessed by authorised personnel involved in league management.


4. Data Deletion


We will retain your personal information for 12 months from the date of submission. After this period, all data will be securely deleted, and no personal information will be retained in our records.


5. Data Security


We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
